Hobbies

My life is a canvas, colored by a diverse array of passions and responsibilities. Drawing, photography, and graphic design allow me to express my creativity with strokes and pixels, crafting visual narratives that captivate the imagination. Writing serves as my inkwell, where I pour out thoughts and stories, weaving words into tapestries of emotion and insight. Alongside these artistic pursuits, my affinity for computer programming and coding fuels my fascination with technology, enabling me to build and innovate in the digital realm. Amidst these endeavors, my greatest joy comes from being a devoted husband for 15 years and a proud father to two remarkable children, Kaleb, 13, and Kaley, 8. They are the cherished keystones of my life, infusing each day with love, laughter, and boundless inspiration.

As a newly inducted member of the Economic Development Committee of the NAACP Houston branch, my aspirations extend beyond mere participation; I envision myself as a catalyst for transformative change and a beacon of leadership within the organization. Embracing the NAACP's legacy of advocating for economic justice and empowerment, I am eager to leverage my skills and experiences to spearhead initiatives that uplift marginalized communities and foster inclusive growth. Through collaborative efforts and strategic planning, I am committed to amplifying the voices of the underserved, advocating for equitable economic policies, and driving tangible progress towards building a more prosperous and equitable future for all. With a steadfast dedication to the NAACP's mission and a fervent belief in the power of collective action, I am poised to not only contribute meaningfully to the Economic Development Committee but also to emerge as a guiding force within the Houston branch, steering our endeavors towards greater impact and relevance in the pursuit of social and economic justice.
